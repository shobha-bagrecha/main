Coaching That Goes Beyond the Surface

Most coaching focuses on strategy and behavior. Shobha's approach goes deeper — integrating the inner world of the leader with the outer demands of performance.

When your nervous system is calm, your mind is clear, and your values are aligned — decision-making becomes sharper, leadership becomes more authentic, and results come with less effort and more fulfillment.

Whether you are a CEO navigating a pivotal transition, a high-potential leader feeling the weight of burnout, or a professional ready to step into a bigger role — this coaching is for you.

How the Coaching Works

A structured yet personalized journey — fully adapted to your situation and goals.

01

Discovery Session

A free 20-minute conversation to understand your situation, goals, and challenges. We'll explore whether coaching is the right fit and discuss the best approach for you.

02

Deep Assessment

We begin by understanding where you are — your strengths, your blocks, your energy patterns, and your core values. This forms the foundation of everything that follows.

03

Breathwork Integration

You'll learn specific breathwork practices tailored to your goals — whether for stress management, mental clarity, energy boosting, or emotional regulation. Tools you can use every day.

04

Coaching Sessions

Regular 1:1 sessions (typically weekly or bi-weekly) to work through your goals, address blockers, build on progress, and develop your leadership edge.

05

Practices & Reflection

Between sessions, you'll apply specific practices and reflection exercises. This is where the real transformation happens — in daily life, not just coaching sessions.

06

Results & Integration

Track measurable shifts in your performance, wellbeing, and leadership. Build an independent practice that continues delivering value long after coaching ends.
