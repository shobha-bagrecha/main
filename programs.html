Evidence-Based Practices

Art of Living Programs

Ancient breathing techniques and meditation practices, validated by modern neuroscience — brought to you by a Senior Faculty with 22+ years of experience.

What Is the Art of Living?

Founded by Sri Sri Ravi Shankar, the Art of Living Foundation is one of the world's largest volunteer-based NGOs, operating in 180+ countries. Its programs blend ancient yogic wisdom with modern scientific validation to help people live with energy, clarity, and joy.

As a Senior Faculty, Shobha has delivered these transformative programs to over 500,000 people across 20+ countries — from corporate boardrooms to conflict zones to rural villages.

The programs are not religious. They are practical, science-backed tools for anyone who wants to perform better, feel better, and live with greater purpose.

Core Programs

Each program is designed for a specific level of practice and life goal.

The Happiness Program

The foundational Art of Living course. Includes the Sudarshan Kriya breathing technique — a powerful rhythmic breathwork practice shown in peer-reviewed studies to reduce cortisol, anxiety, and depression while boosting serotonin and energy levels.

Duration: Typically 3–5 days | Format: In-person or live online

Advanced Meditation Program

A deep dive into meditation, silence, and inner experience. This residential retreat allows participants to disconnect from daily noise and reconnect with their deepest self. Profound and life-changing for most who attend.

Duration: 5–7 days | Format: Residential retreat

Sri Sri Yoga

A holistic approach to yoga that integrates postures, breath, and meditation. Suitable for all levels. Focused on building physical vitality, mental calm, and spiritual awareness — not just flexibility.

Duration: Ongoing weekly sessions | Format: Group or private

Corporate Wellness Programs

Customized programs for organizations. Combines breathwork, stress management, leadership presence, and team dynamics. Delivered as workshops, retreats, or ongoing training series. Used by Fortune 500 companies and government institutions.

Duration: Half-day to multi-day | Format: On-site or virtual

Sahaj Samadhi Meditation

A personalized mantra-based meditation technique. Simple, effortless, and profoundly restorative. Taught one-on-one, this technique allows the mind to access deep states of rest and awareness naturally — without forcing or concentrating.

Duration: 4 sessions | Format: Private instruction

Why These Practices Work

These aren't just ancient traditions — they are increasingly validated by modern science.

🧠

Cortisol Reduction

Studies on Sudarshan Kriya show statistically significant reductions in serum cortisol — the primary stress hormone — after just one session.

💚

Nervous System Regulation

Research published in Frontiers in Human Neuroscience confirms rhythmic breathwork activates the parasympathetic nervous system — shifting you from fight-or-flight to rest-and-restore within minutes.

🎯

Improved Focus & Decision-Making

Harvard Medical School research shows meditation increases gray matter in the prefrontal cortex — the brain region governing executive function, focus, and emotional regulation.

😴

Better Sleep & Recovery

Participants consistently report improved sleep quality and faster recovery from fatigue after completing Art of Living programs — critical for sustained high performance.
